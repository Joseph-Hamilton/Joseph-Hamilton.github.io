Portrait De La Jeune Fille En Feu

Portrait De La Jeune Fille En Feu is a French film made in 2019, its cast is as follows:

  • Director: Céline Sciamma
  • Writer: Céline Sciamma
  • Actress: Noémie Merlant as Marianne
  • Actress: Adèle Haenel as Héloïse
The Cast

In 1790, Bretagne, France, talented young painter Marianne (Noémie Merlant played) received a commision, that she needed finished a portrait of a rich miss Héloïse (Adèle Haenel played) before her marriage without her knowledge. The two people lived with each other in an isolated island. During the day the femail painter quietly observed the lady's every single move and induged in brush in the middle of the night. In close contact with painting and being painted, the two gradually sprouted taboo ambiguous feelings even in an era full of ritual constraints. As the wedding day drawed closer, their love fire was burning hotter.
